Final Chapter

Happy 18th Birthday,
Varsha 💜

18 years ago, a story began. It didn't come with instructions or a map or a clear idea of what it was supposed to become. It just began — one day at a time, one year at a time, one version of you at a time.

✦

And here you are. On the other side of all those years. All those exam nights and lunch breaks and late conversations and songs that understood. All the ordinary days that somehow added up to something.

You are, without question, someone worth knowing. The kind of person who loves things deeply — music, rain, the specific purple of dusk. The kind of person who picks a hard thing to study because they want to build something real. The kind of person who keeps friendships alive through text boxes and years and distance.

✦

I want to be honest: this isn't the ending of anything. It's actually the opposite. 18 is the part where everything you've been building — quietly, without realizing it — becomes the foundation you get to build from.

The music stays. The rain stays. The purple stays. You stay — just with more room to be all of it.

✦

So: happy birthday, Varsha. For real. Not the polite kind — the kind where you actually mean every word of it.

May 18 be the beginning of the best chapter yet.

18 isn't the end of childhood.

It's the beginning of your own story.
