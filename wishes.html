Page Five

18 Wishes For 18 Years

One for every year you've quietly been becoming yourself.

01
✨

More Good Days

May the good days outnumber the hard ones. And on the hard ones, may you remember how many good ones came before.

02
💜

Confidence

The kind that doesn't need anyone's approval. The kind that walks into a room and already knows it belongs there.

03
🌧

More Rainy Days

The kind you love. Window light, the sound of it, that feeling like the world is pausing just for you.

04
🏗

A Future You're Proud Of

Not someone else's version of success. Yours. Built the way you build things — solid, intentional, made to last.

05
🎵

The Right Song At The Right Time

Always. May you always find the song that understands exactly what you're feeling when words won't.

06
🏍

Open Roads

And the freedom to go wherever they lead. May you always have somewhere worth heading and the wind at your back.

07
🌙

Peace At Night

The kind where your brain actually lets you rest. No overthinking. No replaying conversations. Just quiet.

08
🌱

To Keep Growing

Even when it's uncomfortable. Especially then. The version of you at 25 is going to be incredible — may she get to exist.

09
💪

Resilience

Not the kind that pretends it doesn't hurt. The kind that feels it fully and still gets back up anyway.

10
🎤

A BTS Concert

Someday. The kind where you know every word and you're in the crowd and it's exactly as good as you imagined it would be.

11
🤝

Good People Around You

The kind who show up. Who check in. Who make the ordinary days feel less ordinary just by being in them.

12
🗺

Adventures

Big ones, small ones. A road you've never been down. A city you've only seen in pictures. A moment that becomes a story.

13
📖

Clarity

Not always — some confusion is how you figure yourself out. But enough clarity to know which direction to walk in.

14
🌟

Moments Worth Remembering

May you collect them. Not just the big ones — the small ones too. The ones that catch you off guard and stay.

15
🏥

Good Health

The kind you take for granted until you don't have it. May you have it, and may you appreciate it, and may it always come back when it wavers.

16
🎯

Things To Look Forward To

Always at least one. Something on the horizon. Something worth getting through the hard week for. May you always have that.

17
🌸

Kindness — For Others And Yourself

You're usually better at the first one than the second. May you give yourself the same grace you give everyone else.

18
💜

A Really Good 18th Year

Not perfect. Just good. Full of the things that matter. May this year be the beginning of something that turns out to be exactly what you needed.

18 years of being you.

That alone deserves celebrating.
